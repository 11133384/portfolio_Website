Unit 1: Online World

This unit explores the features, benefits, and risks of the online world. You will learn about how the internet works, online communication, digital citizenship, and how to stay safe online.

  • Understanding the structure of the internet and the World Wide Web
  • Types of online services (email, social media, cloud storage, etc.)
  • Online safety and security
  • Digital footprints and privacy
  • Responsible use of online resources
