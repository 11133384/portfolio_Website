Unit 3: Digital Portfolio

This unit focuses on creating and maintaining a digital portfolio to showcase your skills, achievements, and work. You will learn how to organize your work, present it effectively, and use digital tools to build your portfolio.

  • Understanding the purpose and benefits of a digital portfolio
  • Organizing and selecting work for your portfolio
  • Designing and structuring your portfolio
  • Using web tools and platforms to create a digital portfolio
  • Presenting your skills and achievements professionally
  • Keeping your portfolio updated
